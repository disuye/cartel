About

Enthusiast owned & operated workshop based in southern Portugal.

Mechanical restoration, repair & recommissioning of late 1980s, 1990s, and young-timer classic cars. Rebuilt to original factory spec using OEM parts, or 'just get it running' depending on the project.

All European marques catered for but in particular BMW, Mercedes Benz & early AMG, Porsche, Volkswagen and Lotus.

We will happily chat cars all day long, so feel free to contact us.

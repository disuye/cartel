About

Enthusiast owned & operated automotive workshop based in southern Portugal. Our passion is 1980s, 1990s, and young-timer classic cars (up the early 2000s).

All European marques are catered for but we specifically know our E-series BMWs (if you want to get really specific then E30 M3, E30 320is, E34 M5; the S14 & S38 powered cars). We've dabbled with Porsche (fore & aft engined), Mercedes Benz (early AMG and Cosworth), some Volkswagen, Volvo, Audi, Lancia, and turn of the century Lotus.

We do the work... You do the driving.

Rebuilt to original factory spec using OEM parts, or 'just get it running' depending on the budget... Our space is dedicated to mechanical restoration, troubleshooting & repair, and general vehicle recommissioning. Helping enthusiast car owners keep their cars on the road:

  • Periodic and interval servicing.
  • Replacing, upgrading or remanufacturing worn parts.
  • Prepping a garage queen for the summer season or simply ensuring your beloved weekend thrasher is in tip-top condition.

Cartel Automotive is not, however, a full restoration shop. We prefer to avoid bodywork and paint – it's not our skill set – and our involvement with interior & upholstery extends to swapping out parts. Having said that, we are happy to advise & co-manage projects alongside a vehicle's owner. There are many other workshops around Portugal well established for complete nut & bolt restoration projects, and we happily defer to them.

Anyway! We will chat cars all day long so feel free to drop us a line, either with project requests or just to say hello.
